Gear Gurus is your local mobile bike repair service, proudly operating throughout the greater St. George area. We specialize in expert, on-the-go maintenance and repairs. We offer a convenient way to bring the bike shop to you, whether you’re at home, at work, or on your commute, so you can spend less time waiting and more time out riding. Our mechanics are friendly and well-trained in all aspects of bike repair, from flat repairs to suspension services to full bike overhauls. Our goal is simple: to make bike care convenient, professional, and rider-focused. With years of hands-on experience, our friendly and certified mechanics tackle everything from quick fixes to complex repairs. Whether it’s a flat tire, a squeaky drivetrain, a suspension service, or a full bike overhaul, we’ve got you covered. We service all types of bikes, road, mountain, gravel, commuter, and e-bikes, and we welcome riders of all levels. Whether you're an experienced cyclist training for a race, an enthusiast gearing up for a weekend ride, or a daily commuter where your bike is your main form of transportation, Gear Gurus is here to keep your ride safe and reliable. No more hauling your bike to a shop or waiting days to get your bike back, we provide onsite repairs where and when you need them.
